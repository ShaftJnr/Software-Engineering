About Us

SANTEO HOSTEL is located in Tesano, directly across from the historic Unidaz hostel. It was constructed on April 11, 2002.

It offers around fifty rooms for both working and studying individuals, as well as a wide parking lot, making it an ideal location for individuals who own automobiles. In the backyard, there is a basketball court and a tennis court as well.

Additionally, there is free internet for everyone. There are five kitchens and two large halls. Each room has its own bathroom as well as two huge closets. Some rooms have either air conditioning or a fan.These accommodations are quite inexpensive.

Santeo Hostel Started as an unkown building but has come to be known as one of the most reputable hostels among others such as Glass Hostel, Bethel Hostel, Delicious Hostel, Palace hostel and many others in the Tesano area.
